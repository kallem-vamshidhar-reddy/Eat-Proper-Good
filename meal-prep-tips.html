Meal Prep Tips for Busy People

Save time and eat healthier with these easy meal prep tips from Eat Proper Good. Plan your meals ahead and ensure you’re always ready with wholesome meals.

Top Meal Prep Tips

  • Start with a grocery list to avoid impulse buys.
  • Use large containers to store multiple portions for efficiency.
  • Focus on balanced meals: protein, veggies, and carbs.
  • Pre-cook grains, proteins, and veggies in bulk.
  • Invest in a high-quality food storage system that keeps meals fresh.
  • Consider freezing meals for longer storage.
  • Always label your meal prep containers with the date.

Advanced Meal Prep Strategies

If you’re ready to take your meal prep to the next level, here are some advanced strategies:

  • Batch cook for the entire week: Dedicate one day for all meal prepping.
  • Invest in a slow cooker or Instant Pot for hands-free cooking.
  • Pre-portion snacks, sauces, and dressings to avoid overeating.
  • Use mason jars for salads and overnight oats for easy grab-and-go meals.
  • Incorporate a variety of cuisines to keep meals exciting.

Simple Meal Prep Recipes

1. Chicken and Veggie Stir-Fry

This simple stir-fry is a perfect meal prep recipe. Packed with lean protein and fiber-rich veggies, it’s a healthy and easy-to-make option for your week.

  • Ingredients: Chicken breast, bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, soy sauce, olive oil
  • Directions: Cook the chicken, stir-fry the veggies, and combine with soy sauce. Serve with brown rice.

2. Overnight Oats

Prepare these oats the night before, and you’ll have a nutritious breakfast ready to go. Add fruit, nuts, and seeds for a custom flavor.

  • Ingredients: Rolled oats, almond milk, chia seeds, honey, berries
  • Directions: Combine all ingredients in a jar and refrigerate overnight. Top with your favorite fruits.
